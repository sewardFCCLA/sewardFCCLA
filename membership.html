Membership Information

HOW TO JOIN FCCLA

To join, you must gain membership from our advisor, Mrs. Brandenburgh.

FEES: $20 Due to the office

BENEFITS OF JOINING FCCLA

LEARN WITH FRIENDS: Join FCCLA to learn leadership skills and serve your community with friends.

WHEN TO JOIN: Students can join FCCLA in 9th Grade all the way up to 12th grade.

GET RECOGNITION: Students can gain recognition in STAR events and qualify for the FCCLA National Convention.

OUR SERVICE

Our FCCLA members attended the Fall Leadership Conference in October.

We painted pumpkins for our community around Halloween.

To kick off the holiday season, our FCCLA chapter and the Seward High Key Club went to a local nursing home to decorate cookies with the residents.

Before Christmas, FCCLA hosted Breakfast With Santa at our local event center.
